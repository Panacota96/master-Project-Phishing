## Why Now

1. **NIS2 Directive** (transposed into French law by late 2024) extends cybersecurity obligations to a broader set of entities — including educational institutions that supply engineers to critical sectors.
2. **AI-generated phishing** (GPT-4/5-powered spear phishing) is rendering outdated "spot the typo" training obsolete. Platforms need real, diverse, signal-rich corpora — not generated text.
3. **GDPR enforcement acceleration** — French CNIL fines have increased 340% since 2022. Institutions are scrutinizing data processors more carefully; a GDPR-native architecture is now a procurement requirement, not a differentiator.
4. **Engineering school enrollment** in France is growing — ~90,000 students per year graduate from Grandes Ecoles. That cohort enters companies without standardized cybersecurity training.
